Cancellation & Refund Policy

Cancellation: A transaction is automatically cancelled if payment is not completed within 45 seconds of the QR code being generated or if you have click the cancel button manually.

Refund Eligibility: Refunds are only initiated if: Payment was successful (money deducted), but the machine failed to dispense the product due to a mechanical or technical error.

Non-Refundable: No refunds are provided for "change of mind" once the dispensing process has started.

Process: To request a refund, please contact us within 24 hours with the Transaction ID. Verified refunds are processed within 2-3 business days.
